# Peter Knight

Peter Knight is a Professor of American Studies at the University of Manchester. He has directed and co-directed several international initiatives on the topic of conspiracy theories.

### Key Roles & Projects
* **Routledge Handbook of Conspiracy Theories**: Co-edited this handbook with Michael Butter.
* **Comparative Analysis of Conspiracy Theories in Europe**: Directed this interdisciplinary network involving 130 scholars from EU academic institutions.
* **Conspiracy Theories Book Series**: Serves as a Series Editor for this ongoing Routledge publication.

### Selected Bibliography
* *Conspiracy culture: from Kennedy to the X Files* (2013).
* *Routledge Handbook of Conspiracy Theories* (2020).
